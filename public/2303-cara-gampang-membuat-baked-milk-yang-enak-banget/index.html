Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang dibutuhkan dalam pembuatan Baked milk:
  1. Gunakan 2 butir kuning telur
  2. Gunakan 500 ml susu cair
  3. Sediakan 5 sdm tepung maizena
  4. Ambil 3 sdm gula pasir

For delicious creamy flavor, this Russian milk is baked until it has a caramel color and thicker consistency. Stream Tracks and Playlists from Baked Milk on your desktop or mobile device. Russian baked milk, known as ryazhenka, somewhat resembles sweetened condensed milk. The initial slow heat gives the milk a dense, caramelized quality, and a rich creamy color.

Cara membuat Baked milk:
  1. Masukkan semua bahan tadi (kuning telur, susu cair, tepung maizena dan gula pasir) aduk rata
  2. Saring campuran bahan tadi dan masak dengan api kecil sampai mengental. Di aduk terus ya biar gk gosong
  3. Lalu pindahkan ke wadah dan ratakan.
  4. Masukkan ke freezer kurang lebih 2jam
  5. Setelah itu potong sesuai selera, lalu olesi dengan kuning telur
  6. Panggang di suhu 200 derajat selama 20-25 menit

Cool baked milk in refrigerator in order for the fat to harden so you can remove it. (This step isn't necessary but it makes the texture of ryazhenka creamier and more appealing).
